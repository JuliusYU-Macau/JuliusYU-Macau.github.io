About me

Ziyue Yu is a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Applied Technology at Macao Polytechnic University (MPU), jointly advised by Prof. Giovanni Pau from University of Bologna / University of California, Los Angeles, and Dr. Wuman Luo, Prof. Tansim Tse from MPU. He got master and bachelor degree from Hong Kong Baptist University and Zhejiang Chinese Medical University, respectively. His interest is data mining in healthcare (e.g., disease risk prediction).
